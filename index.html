About Our Business

Smish-it protects users from SMS phishing by checking links in text messages against trusted threat databases in real time. It’s designed for anyone—from seniors to tech professionals—who wants quick, reliable feedback on suspicious messages. By automating link analysis, Smish-it reduces stress, prevents scams, and keeps personal information safe.

How It Works

+

Step 1: Suspicious Message

User receives a strange or unrecognized text with a link.

+

Step 2: Smish-It Scans

The link is analyzed in real-time for known threats and phishing patterns.

+

Step 3: Safe or Unsafe Verdict

Smish-It instantly provides the user a result — safe to click or not.
